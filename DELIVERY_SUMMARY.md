## 🎓 Tips & Tricks

**Drawing Better Polygons:**
- Click 3+ points to create polygon
- Click close to first point to close
- Blue outline shows what you're drawing

**Drawing Better Circles:**
- Click for center
- Move mouse away to see radius
- Larger movements = larger circles
- Click to confirm

**Testing Effectively:**
- Start simple, add complexity
- Test edge cases
- Try multiple scenarios
- Compare results
